FS-Quiz Testquiz 2024

This is the section of the test quiz. The test quiz will take place on the 14 of January at 3pm CET. The quiz consists of new questions that are in the style of typical registration questions. The quiz will be about 90 minutes long and the level of difficulty will be in the direction of FSG. The questions have to be answered one after the other in a given time. The number of correct answers is more important than the total time. The ranking and the correct answers will be published one hour after the quiz.
To participate in the quiz you have to register your team first. This is possible until shortly before the quiz.
